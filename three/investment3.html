Here is how it works:

Step 1

The investor clicks on the Endowment Spinner and it lands on $10.

Step 2

He/she then decides to place $5 (of the $10 endowment) in the general fund, and to keep the rest for him/herself.

Step 3

The investor clicks on the Muliplier Spinner and it lands on 3, so that the $5 that the investor placed in the general fund is now $15. Meaning that there is now $15 in the muture fund.

Step 4

Control over the contents of the mature fund now passes to the executor, so that he/she now has $15. In this example, he/she decides to return $5 of the mature fund to the investor, and to keep the rest for him/herself. Now both the investor and the executor have $10 dollars. If this round is selected as “the round that counts” it will mean that both participants will have a 1 in 7 chance to winning a $10 Amazon gift card. You will know which round has been selected as the round that counts as you have completed all five of the tasks.

Click “Next” to find out if you have been assigned to playing the role of Investor or Executor.
